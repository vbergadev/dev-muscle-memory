# DSA — Loiane Groner's book track

A **mandatory** parallel track: one chapter per week from the book **"Learning JavaScript Data Structures and Algorithms"** (Loiane Groner). Read + **implement by hand** every structure/algorithm, in TypeScript.

Author's companion repo (consult **only after** trying on your own): <https://github.com/loiane/javascript-datastructures-algorithms>

| Ch | Folder | Topic | Study week |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | `ch-01-js-overview` | JS overview | 1 |
| 02 | `ch-02-arrays` | Arrays | 1 |
| 03 | `ch-03-stacks` | Stacks | 2 |
| 04 | `ch-04-queues-deques` | Queues and deques | 3 |
| 05 | `ch-05-linked-lists` | Linked lists | 4 |
| 06 | `ch-06-sets` | Sets | 5 |
| 07 | `ch-07-dictionaries-hashes` | Dictionaries and hashes | 6 |
| 08 | `ch-08-recursion` | Recursion | 7 |
| 09 | `ch-09-trees` | Trees | 8 |
| 10 | `ch-10-graphs` | Graphs | 9 |
| 11 | `ch-11-sorting-searching` | Sorting and searching | 10 |
| 12 | `ch-12-algorithm-patterns` | Patterns (greedy, divide & conquer) | 11 |
| 13–15 | `ch-13-15-advanced` | Advanced algorithms, FP, Big-O | 12 |

Each chapter is a TS workspace with Vitest. Every chapter ships starter stubs + failing tests — make them pass, then extend on your own.
